Is my data actually safe? +
Yes. All analysis happens inside your browser ‚Äî your reports are never uploaded to our servers. We don't store, see, or transmit your Seller Central data. When you close the tab, it's gone.
Which reports do I need to download from Seller Central? +
It depends on what you're analyzing. For reimbursements: FBA Inventory Ledger + Settlement Report. For PPC: Search Term Report. For inventory: Business Report + FBA Inventory Age. We show you exactly where to find each one when you select your analysis.
How accurate are the reimbursement findings? +
Very accurate for cases that appear clearly in your FBA Inventory Ledger. We flag cases where your ledger shows a loss event (damaged, lost, or returned) without a corresponding reimbursement entry. Your own Seller Central data is the source of truth ‚Äî we're just reading it and highlighting what may need attention.
Will Amazon penalize me for filing reimbursement claims? +
No. Requesting reimbursement for FBA fulfillment discrepancies is explicitly covered under their FBA Seller Policy. Sellers have an 18-month window to file claims. Amazon expects sellers to audit and file ‚Äî it's a standard part of selling on FBA. Filing legitimate claims is a normal part of the FBA seller experience.
Is this really free? What's the catch? +
Completely free during our beta period. We're building a product for Indian Amazon sellers and need real-world feedback. Your honest feedback after using the tool (via the rating widget after your results) is what makes this possible. We'll introduce paid tiers eventually for advanced features ‚Äî but the core analysis will always have a free option.

01

Reimbursement detection

In the FBA fulfillment process, units can occasionally be lost, damaged, or have fee discrepancies. These events are recorded in your Inventory Ledger, but reimbursements aren't always automatic. We surface every eligible case ‚Äî with the exact claim template you paste into Seller Support.

02

FBA fee audit

FBA weight handling fees are based on measured dimensions ‚Äî which can sometimes differ from what you declared. If there's a measurement discrepancy, fees may be higher than expected on every order. We cross-reference your settlement data to flag potential fee discrepancies and tell you exactly what to verify.
