Amazon FBA Reimbursements: Money You're Missing

Read Time: 2–3 Minutes

Many Amazon FBA sellers in India lose 1–3% of annual revenue due to unclaimed reimbursements. Lost inventory, damaged returns, and incorrect fee charges often go unnoticed.

1. Lost or Damaged Inventory

Amazon occasionally misplaces or damages units in fulfillment centers.

Action: Audit inventory adjustment reports monthly.

2. Customer Return Errors

Refunds are sometimes issued without the item being returned.

Check: Compare return reports with received inventory.

3. Incorrect Weight & Fee Charges

Incorrect product dimensions can inflate FBA fees.

Fix: Regularly review fee preview reports.

4. Removal & Disposal Errors

Items marked disposed may still be sellable.

How to Recover Reimbursements

  • Download transaction reports monthly
  • Audit inventory adjustments
  • Track return discrepancies
  • File cases within claim windows

Why This Matters

Even a ₹10–15 lakh seller can recover ₹50,000–₹2,00,000 annually through systematic FBA audits.

Final Thoughts

Amazon reimbursements are often overlooked profit leaks. Regular audits ensure you recover what you’re owed.
