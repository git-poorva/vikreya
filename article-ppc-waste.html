Why Your Amazon PPC Budget Is Being Wasted

Read Time: 3 Minutes

Amazon PPC can scale your brand — or quietly drain profits. Many sellers in India overspend without structured optimization, resulting in high ACoS and low ROI.

1. Running Broad Campaigns Without Negatives

Broad match keywords without negative targeting lead to irrelevant clicks.

Fix: Regularly mine search term reports and add negative keywords weekly.

2. Ignoring ACoS vs TACoS

ACoS measures ad efficiency, but TACoS shows overall business health.

Healthy Benchmark: Lower TACoS over time as organic sales grow.

3. No Campaign Structure

Mixing auto, broad, phrase, and exact in one campaign limits control.

Best Practice:

  • Auto for discovery
  • Manual broad for expansion
  • Exact for scaling

4. Scaling Too Fast

Increasing budgets aggressively without data can spike ACoS.

Tip: Increase bids gradually (10–15%) after consistent conversions.

5. Not Optimizing Product Listings

PPC sends traffic — but listings convert sales. Poor images or weak copy increase wasted spend.

Smart PPC Strategy

  • Optimize weekly
  • Track keyword-level performance
  • Improve conversion rate before scaling ads
  • Reduce wasted ad spend continuously

Final Thoughts

Amazon PPC is a performance channel. Structured optimization lowers ACoS and improves profitability long-term.
